## Typography
- **Display**: Montserrat, 800 weight — hero headlines
- **Headings**: Montserrat, 700 — section titles
- **Body**: Inter, 400/500 — paragraphs
- **Accent text**: Montserrat, 600 — labels, tags

## Layout
- Max width: 1280px, centered
- Section padding: 100px vertical
- Grid: 12-col, responsive

## WOW Effects
1. **Hero**: Animated canvas particle network (neurons connecting), dark bg with subtle red glow
2. **Typing animation**: Hero headline cycles through services
3. **Scroll reveal**: Sections fade+slide in on scroll (Intersection Observer)
4. **Service cards**: Red neon glow border on hover + scale
5. **Counters**: Animated number count-up on scroll into view
6. **Parallax**: Hero background subtle parallax on mouse move
7. **CTA buttons**: Ripple effect on click + glow pulse animation

## Components
- Glassmorphism cards: rgba(255,255,255,0.04) backdrop-blur
- Red accent line decorators on section headings
- Dot/line geometric decorators in corners
